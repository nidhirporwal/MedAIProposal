AI-Powered Medical Billing Transparency

Transforming healthcare costs into clear, actionable information for every patient

Main Issue

  • Medical billing in the United States lacks consistency and transparency, leaving patients vulnerable to unpredictable and inflated costs.
  • Itemized bills often contain complex codes and prices that differ dramatically from one provider to another, making it difficult to judge fairness.
  • Without the right knowledge or tools, many patients overpay for healthcare services, unaware that they can challenge or reduce these charges.

Proposed Solution

  • Develop an AI-driven application that can scan and interpret medical receipts (photos or PDFs) using advanced OCR technology.
  • Cross-reference each line item with trusted Medicare benchmarks, adjusted for regional pricing variations.
  • Highlight any charges that are significantly higher than the benchmark, providing clear, data-backed justification.
  • Automatically generate professional, personalized dispute letters to help patients negotiate or contest unfair charges.
  • Offer an intuitive interface that explains results in plain language, making it accessible to non-experts.

Step-by-Step Plan

Gather Information and Tools Compile a diverse range of sample receipts, identify commonly overcharged services, and secure a comprehensive Medicare pricing database.
System Setup Integrate OCR engines and AI models to ensure accurate reading, interpretation, and classification of billing data.
Extract Data Build seamless upload functionality and ensure the system organizes extracted text into a clean, structured format.
Price Comparison Implement automated comparison logic to flag anomalies, factoring in geographic pricing adjustments.
User Interface Design a clear, visually engaging dashboard that highlights issues and allows direct action, like dispute generation.
Privacy & Security Adhere to HIPAA standards, anonymize data where possible, and secure explicit user consent.
Launch & Iterate Deploy the platform, gather user feedback, update pricing data regularly, and refine AI accuracy over time.
